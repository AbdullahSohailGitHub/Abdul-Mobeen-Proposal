Live Commerce Experience

Designing for real-time, high-urgency commerce

⚡

Urgency without anxiety

Flash-sale and limited-drop flows on Shopify Plus projects taught me how to create urgency that converts — without making users feel pressured into mistakes. The same UX tension applies to live selling.

🎬

Video-dominant viewport thinking

For Atelier RMR and Senbird Tea, I designed hero sections where video or motion is the primary storytelling element. Scroll behaviour, CTA placement, and layout logic around moving content — all handled.

Multi-market, multi-stream architecture
